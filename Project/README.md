## Usage

Upon running the program, you'll be presented with a menu of options:

1. **Add Product:** Allows you to add a new product to the inventory by providing its name and quantity.
2. **Update Quantity:** Lets you update the quantity of an existing product by entering its name and the new quantity.
3. **Display Inventory:** Shows the current inventory with product names and their respective quantities.
4. **Exit:** Exits the program.

Follow the on-screen prompts to interact with the program and choose the desired option.
